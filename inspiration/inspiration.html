My Inspirations

By Hudson Moss


Sukamii By John Hollins:

Sukamii's Instagram

Sukamii is a Brand that draws motivation from fashion icons like Nigo and Takashi Murakami. Im drawn to the brand because I feel like Mr. Hollins creates a very cool brand identity.

He keeps this brand identity by using the same base character and changine colors, materials, and shape of the character to make each hoodie or t shirt different but still on brand.

Mr. Hollins also creates a lot of plushes and figures that can be great decorations!


Offgod/ Bandage Boy by Andrew Mok:

Andrew Mok's Instagram

Andrew mock is one of my favorite artists that creates figures and statues. Hes just 19 and already accomplished so many things in the design ans fashion world. He motivates me to

start creating more and to get my work out there. I want to get better at 3D modeling so I can create cool figures and other random things. Andrew also has a clothing brand named

Bandage Boy where he creates some very unique clothing.


Nevercontent.jp:

Never Content's Instagram
Never Content Website

I really like how this artist combines 90s videogame-styled graphics with the automotive scene. Their work captures the 90s Japanese drift scene mood perfectly. The use of bright

and bold colors, attention to detail, and grainy 90s graphics create the perfect mood. This account really inspires me because I love both 90s video games and cars. This gives me hope

that I can end up having a job that combines design and cars. I'm really into learning about the 90s car culture. Specifically, the 90s drifting scene when it all started.
